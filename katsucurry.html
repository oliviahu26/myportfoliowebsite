How To Make Katsu Curry


I photographed and documented the step by step process of my roommate and I cooking chicken katsu curry together. It has become tradition for us to cook dinner together whenever we could. After a long day at school, it's nice to come back and create something delicious to enjoy together. To promote cooking with others, I added a recipe card inside.
