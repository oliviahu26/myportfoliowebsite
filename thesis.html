What Am I Feeling?

For my senior year thesis, I created a series of seven unique booklets, each devoted to capturing the essence of human emotions: happiness, anger, sadness, fear, surprise, disgust, and bad. These emotions are derived from the Feelings Wheel widely utilized by mental health professionals. With an introduction zine, shows the purpose for creating this project, shows glimpses of each booklet, and is a guide for viewers on how to experience each booklet individually.
